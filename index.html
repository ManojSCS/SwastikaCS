FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide online accounting services?

Yes. We provide fully online accounting, GST filing, TDS, EPF/ESIC and compliance services for businesses across India. You don't need to visit our office.

How do I buy a package from this website?

Click "Buy Now" on any package card. You'll be asked to create an account (or login), then proceed to payment via UPI, Net Banking, or Debit/Credit Card. Once confirmed, our team contacts you within 24 hours.

Can you handle both GST and TDS together?

Yes. You can purchase individual packages (GST — ₹5,000/yr and TDS/TCS — ₹5,000/yr) or opt for our Standard or All Accounting packages which include both services.

What documents do I need to provide?

After purchase, our team will send you a document checklist based on your package. Generally, you'll need your GSTIN, PAN, bank statements, and purchase/sale invoices for accounting work.

Is my financial data safe with you?

Absolutely. We maintain strict confidentiality. All client data is stored securely and is never shared with third parties under any circumstances.

Do you issue a receipt after payment?

Yes. A payment confirmation with order ID is generated immediately after successful payment. A formal invoice/receipt is shared on your registered email within 24 hours.
